LOCATION:
Topeka, Kansas
OVERVIEW:

MALA LLC is a Design Build company that creates a complete solution for individual landscape visions. We believe in the design and building process, which allows for a seamless transition to occur from the drawing to the finished project. Landscape, irrigation, hardscape, design and maintenance are all services offered by MALA.

RESULTS:

MALA's new identity is based around the concept of organic structures. Landscape architecture is combining organic, natural areas into something structural but maintaining beauty. The designs and layouts are designed around that same principle.
